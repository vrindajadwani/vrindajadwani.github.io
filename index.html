Her Life History

She was born and brought up in Rajasthan. Later on she settled in Indore. She completed her High School from Indore Public School(IPS), Indore in 2019, science stream. As of now, she's pursuing B.Tech from SGSITS, Indore in the field of Electronics and Telecommunication Engineering.

Poorva's Hobbies

She is a passionate learner and an enthusiast. Apart from being a bright student, she has got many hobbies. She loves drawing and sketching and has mastered the art of making portraits. Her drawings are a masterpiece!. She is interested in music as well and plays the guitar. She is always ready to take up difficult songs!. Also, she is good at debating. She often participates in debate competitions and leads her team to the winning side. She is an avid reader. She enjoys reading fictions like Harry Potter!

A Short Poem (Credits- Abimbola T. Alabi)

You always answer when I call

And help me up if I should fall,

But you never complain at all

You confront me when I am wrong

But will never scold me for long,

Instead, you try to keep me strong

You know the funny things to say

To make me laugh my fears away.

Like the sun, you brighten my day

You see in me gifts I deny

And urge me to give things a try.

You spread for me my wings to fly

You always perceive what I need

And offer it before I plead.

Just like a book, my mind you read

You value little things I do

But won't brag of what you do too.

How can I ever repay you?

And greatest of all I have found

When times are tough and I'm down,

You are the one who sticks around,

My true friend.
